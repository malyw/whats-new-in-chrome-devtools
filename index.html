Recording video in Chrome!

  • Demo

Recently filmstrip was added to Chrome which added ability to record page screenshots when you are recording timeline in Chrome

I created a simple demo which creates video from recorded timeline data (check the "Demo").

It can be really useful for:

  1. Creating video of page loading (to check first paint time, optimize images loading etc.)
  2. To make a video of existing problems (for QA)
